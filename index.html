Every Donation Counts! Do your part in fighting Covid 19

    Provide a new life to help India stand again

    At the onset of the pandemic last year, when hospitals were facing a dire need for protective gear and equipment, we at Serve smile foundation had responded in record time to stand strong beside our saviours, our healthcare heroes. And we were able to fill the gap by raising ₹28+ lakhs through this mission and helped those who were risking their lives to save ours. The record increase of COVID positive patients and fatalities per day, lack of life-saving equipment like oxygen cylinders and concentrators, ventilators and Bipaps is crippling our healthcare infrastructure. And now we are aiming to help the children who have suffered drastically from the covid pandemic in any way possible.


    Providing life changing opportunities:

    By providing basic facilities like shelter ,healthy and hygenic food and quality education we aspier to change the sudden drastic changes which covid has brought.Providing one child all these needs costs approximately ₹60,000. By providing a safr shelter and seamless education which are approximately going to cost about 2 lakhs INR a year.
